Method, confidence & honesty

How this was built — and what it can't claim

This slide is a permanent part of every deck. It states the data tier, the estimate policy, and the reads that stayed honest nulls.

Public-data audit. Everything here was gathered with the same external lens applied to competitors — ad libraries, public social profiles, site crawl, SERP checks, public review/GBP surfaces, traffic estimators. Spend, ROAS, CPA, traffic and ER figures are modelled proxies (ad volume, longevity, offer/LP analysis, public estimators), always labelled EST — never presented as account-verified numbers. Local-pack findings are city-modifier SERP proxies (position bands), not geo-grid measurement.
